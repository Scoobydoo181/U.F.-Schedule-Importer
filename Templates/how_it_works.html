How it works

Log in to ONE.UF

Sign in with your GatorLink username and password and navigate to the student page. Then click on "Register/View Schedule".


View schedule

Click on "Go to my schedule" under the current term.


Download your schedule

Click on the link in the top right corner that says "Download to my calendar". This will prompt you to save a file named "UFSchedule.ics" to your hard drive. Save it somewhere you'll rememeber it.


Upload the file to Google Calendar

Simply add the calendar file and our site will handle the rest.
